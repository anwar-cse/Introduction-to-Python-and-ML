🔹 Tuple কী?

👉 Tuple = List-এর মতোই collection, কিন্তু immutable (change করা যায় না)

Ex.
    t = (1, 2, 3)
    🔹 Tuple-এর বৈশিষ্ট্য

                ✔ ordered (index আছে)
                ✔ immutable (change করা যায় না ❗)
                ✔ duplicate allowed

🔹 Tuple তৈরি করা
                t1 = (1, 2, 3)
                t2 = ("a", "b", "c")
                t3 = (1, "hi", 3.5)

👉 single element:
                    t = (5,)   # comma must ❗
